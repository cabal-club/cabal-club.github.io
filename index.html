ABOUT cabal

cabal is an open source, open protocol, community steered, private peer-to-peer chat platform for people and their communities.

A cabal is a secret key (like cabal://7d99b453506b9743bf5e71fe749f66...) that you share with your friends. This key lets them find the cabal and read & write messages to any of the channels on it. Each cabal is unique and is undiscoverable to those without the key.

Cabal works over the internet, but also lets you chat over the local network. If offline, you can still browse your full chat history, and send messages that will be sync'd with any future peers you connect to.
