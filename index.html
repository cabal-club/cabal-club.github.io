ABOUT cabal

Cabal is a decentralized chat platform for people and their communities. Decentralized means it doesn't rely on servers, so it can never go down. Cabals are private and secure, so those without the secret key can't find it or see your messages.

Cabal is an open source platform that anybody can build on, and is steered by its community.

A cabal is identified by its secret key (like cabal://7d99b453506b9743bf5e71fe749f66...) that you share with your friends. This key lets them find the cabal and read & write messages to any of the channels on it. Each cabal is unique and is undiscoverable to those without the key.

No servers are needed to join a cabal, or to start your own. Everything is stored and runs locally.

Cabal works over the internet, but also lets you chat over the local network. If offline, you can still browse your full chat history, and send messages that will be sync'd with any future peers you connect to.
